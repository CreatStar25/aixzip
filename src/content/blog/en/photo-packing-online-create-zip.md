## 3 Steps to Zip Photos Online

The process is the same for phones and computers.

### Step 1: Open the Tool

No registration required. Just visit our **Zip Creator** page in Chrome, Safari, or Edge. Clean interface, no confusing buttons.

### Step 2: Select Photos

Click the central "Select Files" button.

*   **Mobile**: It opens your gallery. You can swipe to select hundreds of photos at once.
*   **PC**: Hold `Ctrl` (Windows) or `Command` (Mac) to select multiple files.

Since processing is **local**, there's **no uploading time**. Even hundreds of HD photos are added instantly.

### Step 3: Create & Download

Click "Create ZIP".

The system generates a standard ZIP file instantly.
*   **iOS**: Saved to "Files" -> "Downloads".
*   **Android**: Saved to "Download" folder.
*   **PC**: Saved to your chosen path.
